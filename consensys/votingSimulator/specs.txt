	Number of voters
	Number of preferences
	Number of representatives
	Number of proposals voted on

	Voter satisfaction

	Signed vs unsigned intensities
	Complete vs incomplete representative preferences
	Complete vs incomplete voter preferences
	Perfect vs imperfect reps (error)
	Voter to representative matching priority (similarity or polarization)
	Singular vs spread voting weight
	Delegates playing both sides*
